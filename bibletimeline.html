How to Use This Timeline

Click any name below to see who that person was, why they matter, and how they connect to other biblical figures. The eras move from earliest figures to later prophets, kings, exile, and Persian-period people.

Some biblical people are easy to place exactly in time; others, like Job, are harder to date precisely. This page places them in the most reasonable biblical context for learning and study.
